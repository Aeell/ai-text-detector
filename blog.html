How to Spot AI-Generated Homework

With AI tools like ChatGPT and DeepSeek becoming more accessible, students are increasingly using them to complete homework. Here’s how teachers and parents can spot AI-generated work:

  • Uniform Sentence Length: AI often produces text with consistent sentence lengths (10-15 words). Human writing varies more naturally.
  • Polished Phrasing: AI avoids slang and errors, delivering overly neat prose that feels "too perfect" for a 6th-9th grader.
  • Overused Transitions: Look for phrases like "for example," "in conclusion," or "finally"—AI loves structuring text predictably.
  • Lack of Personal Touch: AI rarely includes personal anecdotes or quirky details a student might add.
  • Repetition of Ideas: AI may subtly repeat concepts in a formulaic way, unlike human creativity.
